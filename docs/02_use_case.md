# Use case chinh

## 1. Dat phong
- Input: khach hang, thoi gian, so nguoi, ghi chu.
- Kiem tra phong trong -> tao Booking.
- Gan phong (BookingRooms) va giu phong.

## 2. Check-in
- Chon Booking dang cho.
- Cap nhat trang thai BookingRooms -> Occupied.
- Cap nhat trang thai Room -> Occupied.

## 3. Check-out
- Tong hop tien phong theo so dem.
- Tong hop dich vu.
- Lap hoa don va cho thanh toan.

## 4. Quan ly dich vu
- Them sua xoa dich vu.
- Ghi nhan su dung theo Booking.

## 5. Quan ly nhan su va phan quyen
- Tao nhan vien.
- Gan tai khoan va vai tro.
- Kiem soat quyen theo chuc nang.

## 6. Bao cao
- Doanh thu ngay/thang.
- Cong suat phong.
- Ty le huy phong.
